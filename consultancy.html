Bond License

Those who are involved with export-import business often need the facility of bonded warehouse in which dutiable goods may be stored, manipulated, or undergo manufacturing operations without payment of duty. In order to avail the Bonded Warehousing facility, interested companies will have to take a Bond License from the Customs Bond Commissionerate. Bond Licenses are issued and maintained as per the Bonded Warehouse Licensing Rules, 2008.

At SAMC, we provide all the facilities for you to obtain bond license so that your company can have the bonded warehousing facility. Our team of experts is always ready perform the bond licensing process on yoir behalf and ready to complete the process smoothly
